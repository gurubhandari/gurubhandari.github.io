Biography of Guru Prasad Bhandari

Guru Prasad Bhandari is a software engineer at Kristiania University College and works for the ENViSEC project. Bhandari has published several research papers in the fields of software security, fault diagnosis, and machine learning.

Bhandari’s research mainly focuses on software engineering, cybersecurity, and machine learning. In 2015, he received Silver Jubilee Scholarship Award given by the Indian Council for Cultural Relations(ICCR) to pursue PhD in India.
